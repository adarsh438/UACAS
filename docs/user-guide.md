# UACAS User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to UACAS (University Accreditation and Compliance Automation System). UACAS is an enterprise-grade platform designed specifically for higher education institutions in India to manage their NAAC SSR (Self-Study Report), NBA, and NIRF data in a secure, automated, and streamlined manner.

## First-Time Setup
When you log in for the first time as an IQAC Coordinator or Super Admin:
1. **Password Change**: You will be forced to change your temporary password.
2. **Setup Wizard**: You will be guided through a 5-step wizard to set up your institution's profile, academic years, departments, programs, and team members.

## Dashboard Overview
The Institutional Dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your accreditation readiness.
- **Stats Grid**: Displays key metrics like total faculty, students, NAAC readiness %, and research output.
- **Getting Started Banner**: If your institution is still setting up, this banner provides a checklist of recommended next steps. Click any step to navigate directly to the relevant module.

## The NAAC Module (Criteria 1-7)
The core of UACAS is the NAAC module, which breaks down the SSR into 7 criteria.
1. **Navigation**: Click on "NAAC Module" in the left sidebar. You will see tabs for C1 through C7.
2. **Data Entry**: Click on a criterion tab (e.g., C3 - Research) to view its sub-sections. Fill out the forms provided.
3. **Saving**: UACAS auto-saves data as you work when you click the "Save" button at the bottom of any form.
4. **Help & Guidance**: Look for the `?` icon next to metrics. Clicking it will reveal a tooltip explaining exactly what data NAAC expects for that specific metric.

## Evidence Vault
NAAC requires documentary evidence for most quantitative and qualitative metrics.
1. Navigate to the **Evidence Vault**.
2. Click **Upload Evidence**.
3. Select the file, and tag it with the corresponding Criterion (e.g., C2), Metric Code (e.g., 2.1.1), and Academic Year.
4. Files are stored securely within your institution's isolated database.

## Report Engine (SSR Generation)
Once your data is complete, you can generate the final SSR.
1. Navigate to the **Report Engine**.
2. Select the academic year.
3. Choose your export format (PDF is recommended for final submission, Word for review, Excel for data analysis).
4. Click **Compile & Download**. The system will aggregate all data across the 7 criteria, calculate tentative scores, and generate a professional, formatted report.

## System Management & Security
- **User Roles**: UACAS supports role-based access control. Faculty members can only see their own department's data, while IQAC Coordinators can see everything.
- **Database Backups**: Admins can generate instant, downloadable backups of the entire database from the System Management tab.
- **Destructive Actions**: Deleting critical records (like a department or the whole university profile) requires typing the name of the entity to prevent accidental data loss.

For further assistance, use the **Help & FAQ** module located at the bottom of the left sidebar.
